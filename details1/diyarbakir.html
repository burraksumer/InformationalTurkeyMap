Diyarbakır

Geographical position: 40 ° 37 ° and 41 ° 20 ° east latitudes and 37 ° 30 ° and 38 ° 43 ° north latitudes.

The population of Diyarbakir is 1.699.901 as of 2017.

Streams: Dicle ırmağı, Ambar çayı, Batman çayı, Medya çayı, Sinek çayı, Göz suyu, Madrap suyu, Sinan suyu, Çüngüş suyu.

Lakes: Devegeçidi barajı, Karakaya barajı, Ortaviran göleti, Gözegöl göleti, Kurt Kaya göleti.

Climate: A hard land and semi-arid highland climate is seen.

Flora: Diyarbakır is located in the steeps between the Taurus mountains. There are oak forests on the step edges in the arid structure. Wild fruit trees are found on the edge of Dicle Ridge. The most common type of oak is the oak tree. On cold winter days, the juniper trees take the place of the bushes. Willow, plane, walnut, poplar and menengic trees are also visible in wetlands.
