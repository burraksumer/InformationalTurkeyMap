Ankara

Geographical position: 39 • 55'02 "North latitude to 32,51'11" East longitude.

Mountains: Köroğlu Dağları, (Yıldırım Dağı), İdris Dağı (Dedekaya Tepesi), Elmadağ (Elmalı Dede Tepesi), Dinek Dağı , Balaban Dağı (Hızır Tepe ).

Plains: Haymana ovası, Ankara ovası, Çubuk ovası, Mürted ovası.

Streams: Kızılırmak 250 km, Sakarya ırmağı 170 km, Ankara suyu.

Lakes: Tuz gölü, Mogan gölü , Emir (Eğmir gölü), Hirfanlı baraj gölü , Sarıyer baraj gölü, Çubuk baraj gölü , Çubuk il baraj gölü, Bayındır baraj gölü , Kurboğazı baraj gölü, Kesikköprü baraj gölü.

Climate: Hard step climate in the south, rainy and temperate climate in the north.

Flora: Ankara generally has a wide steppe area. The salt lake basin carries the most distinctive feature of the step character. The trees of the forests are oak, juniper, larch. To the east of the city are stunted stands and wild fruit trees.
