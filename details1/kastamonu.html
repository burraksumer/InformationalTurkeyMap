Kastamonu

Geographical position: 35 ° 48 "and 42 ° 00" north latitudes and 32 ° 43 "and 34 ° 37" east longitudes.

Streams: Araç çayı, Devrekani çayı, Gökırmak, Devrez çay

Climate: The Black Sea in the north and the Central Anatolian climate in the south are seen.

Flora: Kastamonu is rich in vegetation. The largest area covers forests. There are abundant species such as pine, fir, beech, linden, chestnut, elm, hornbeam, myrtle and poplar around İnebolu and Cide.
