Kars

Geographical position: 42 ° 10 ° and 44 ° 49 ° east latitudes and 39 ° 22 ° and 41 ° 37 ° north latitudes.

Streams: Aras ırmağı, Arpaçay, Kars çayı, Kura ırmağı

Lakes: Çıldır gölü, Aygır gölü, Deniz gölü.

Climate: Eastern Anatolia has a high plateau climate.

Flora: It has been shaped according to the characteristics of natural vegetation cover climate. The area is within the natural forest area. The forests are up to 2800 meters. In Kars, cold steppe fields and dwarfed alpine plant communities take up a great deal of space.
