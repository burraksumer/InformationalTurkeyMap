Antalya

Geographical position: 36 ° o 6 'and 37 ° 27' north latitudes and 29 ° 14 'and 32 ° 27' east longitudes.

Mountains: Akdağ (Sallamaz Tepesi 3.024 m.), Susuz Dağları (Kohu Dağı 2.409 m.), Alacadağ (Tovlak Karlılığı Tepesi 2.336 m.), Bey dağları, Tahtalı Dağı, Geyik Dağları.

Streams: Köprü çayı, Aksu Çayı, Manavgat çayı, Düden Çayı, Dim Çayı, Kargı Çayı, Alara Çayı, Karpuz Çayı, Alağır Çayı.

Lakes: Karagöl sazlığı, Söğüt gölü, Avlan gölü.

Climate: Generally Mediterranean climate prevails.

Flora: In the region, arid plants grow. Maki types commonly called "garig" are common. Among these, laden, thyme grass, lavender flowers and husbands are the most common ones. The uplift leaves the shrubs to leafy trees in sections over 300 meters. These include red-eyed and oak trees. Higher sections include hornbeam, maple, ash, poplar and plane trees.
