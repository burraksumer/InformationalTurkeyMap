İzmir

Geographical position: 26 ° 15 "to 28 ° 20" east longitudes and 37 ° 45 "to 39 ° 15" north latitudes.

Mountains: Aydın Dağları, Menteşe Dağları, Sandıklı dağları, Emir dağı, Domaniç dağı, Ahır dağı.

Streams: Küçük Menderes, Gediz ırmağı, Bakırçay.

Lakes: Alaman gölü, Çakalboğaz gölü, Coyaşır gölü, Göbek Kilice gölü, Akgöl, Karagö

Flora: Trees and trees growing in the Mediterranean climate region in Izmir are widespread. The natural vegetation of the province creates red pine, peanut and cypress. Sections of 500-800 m in size are the main areas. There are plane trees, chestnut, ash, willow, poplar and cranberry trees in the secluded and wetlands.
